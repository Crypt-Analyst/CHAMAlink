The ChamaLink Story
The Beginning

In 2007, when I was just 8 years old, I witnessed my grandmother Joyce Mwato transform our family's financial future through her local Chama. Watching her and later my mother Judith Lily walk miles for those Saturday meetings taught me the incredible power of community-driven wealth building, but also showed me how technology could make their lives easier.

The Vision

At Mount Kenya University, studying computer science, I carried this vision of making chama management easier. Now at 25, ChamaLink digitizes that same spirit of collaboration I witnessed as a child, bringing modern tools including online meetings to traditional practices. I'm making life simple for every mother out there, just like my grandmother and mother.
